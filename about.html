ABOUT

Every day, excessive garbage harms our planet, ecosystems, and public health. This platform was established to prove that despite the waste crisis, the solution can start small. The purpose is to advocate in a direct and effective practice which is the proper waste segregation.

By inspiring and educating individuals with the knowledge of segregation of garbage into biodegradable, recyclable, and hazardous types at the point of disposal, we can drastically reduce the amount of waste released into our environment.
